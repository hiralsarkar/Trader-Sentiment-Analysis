Strategic Insights & Recommendations
Extreme Greed is the alpha regime. Highest win rate (46.5%) and highest avg PnL/trade ($67.9) with the smallest position ($3.1K). Size up 30-50% when F&G enters Extreme Greed - the edge is concentrated in the euphoria tail.
Extreme Fear destroys capital. Worst win rate (37.1%) and worst avg PnL/trade ($34.5) of any regime. Cut size 30-50% when the index enters this bucket. Less is more.
Fix the inverted sizing in Fear. Largest avg position ($7.8K) at only 42.1% win rate is the single biggest capital efficiency problem in the dataset. This should be the #1 rule change.
Fee drag compounds in Fear. Avg fee per trade is $1.50 in Fear vs $0.68 in Extreme Greed - over-trading in bad regimes doubles the fee drag. Fewer, higher-conviction entries needed.
Raw score is a near-zero signal (r = -0.08). The 0-100 index value barely correlates with daily PnL. The categorical regime label - especially Extreme buckets - is the actionable variable, not the number.
Build per-coin, per-regime playbooks. TRUMP: +$81 in Fear vs -$454 in Greed. FARTCOIN: opposite. ETH: +$224 in Fear vs +$55 in Greed. Universal rules miss these signals entirely.
