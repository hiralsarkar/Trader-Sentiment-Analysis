Key Findings & Actionable Recommendations
Key Findings
Data-derived insights from 211K+ trades across 5 sentiment regimes
FINDINGS
Best Regime Extreme Greed is the alpha regime: highest win rate (46.5%) and highest avg PnL/trade ($67.9), paradoxically with the smallest average position size ($3.1K). The edge concentrates in the euphoria tail — not broad "Greed."
Worst Regime Extreme Fear is the weakest: lowest win rate (37.1%) and lowest avg PnL/trade ($34.5). Plain Fear shows the largest avg trade size ($7.8K) despite only 42.1% win rate — an inverted, capital-destructive sizing pattern.
Signal Quality Raw Fear & Greed score barely correlates with daily PnL (r = -0.08). The categorical regime label — especially "Extreme" buckets — is the real signal. The 0-100 number is nearly useless on its own.
Concentration Risk PnL is highly concentrated: the top 5 of 32 accounts generated $6.36M (~62%) of total PnL. Aggregate strategies mask significant per-account divergence and survivorship bias effects.
Actionable Recommendations
How to optimise KPIs using sentiment regime signals
STRATEGY
Size Up in Extreme Greed Win rate (46.5%) and avg PnL/trade ($67.9) are regime highs while current avg size ($3.1K) is the lowest. There is room to increase conviction sizing by 30-50% in this regime without proportional risk increase.
Cut Size in Extreme Fear Lowest win rate (37.1%) and lowest avg PnL/trade ($34.5). Reduce position sizes by 30-50% in Extreme Fear — fewer, smaller entries means lower drawdown with little sacrificed edge.
Fix Inverted Sizing in Fear The largest avg trade size ($7.8K) occurs in plain Fear with only 42.1% win rate — the worst possible combination. This is the single biggest capital efficiency problem to address.
Reduce Fee Drag in Fear Avg fee per trade is highest in Fear ($1.50 vs $0.68 in Extreme Greed), indicating over-trading. Fewer, higher-conviction entries in bearish regimes cuts fee drag significantly.
Per-Coin Regime Playbooks Same coin, opposite signal: TRUMP shows +$81 avg PnL in Fear vs -$454 in Greed; FARTCOIN shows -$98 in Fear vs +$19 in Greed. Build per-coin, per-regime rules — not universal strategies.
Audit Bottom-5 Accounts The bottom 5 accounts are consistent PnL drags while the top 5 drive ~62% of profit. Reduce their allocation or retrain their approach. A portfolio rebalancing would materially improve aggregate PnL.
